# 疑问句的基础

## 22 含 be 动词的疑问句

- 含 be 动词的疑问句，就是用 be 动词开头

```markdown
You are hungry. -> Are you hungry?
```

- 主语是第三人称单数时，句子用 Is 开头

```markdown
She is a teacher. -> Is she a teacher?
```

- I am 对应的疑问句为 Am I...

```markdown
I am late. -> Am I late?
```

## 23 回答含 be 动词的疑问句的方法

```markdown
Are you ...?
Yes, I am.
No, I am not. / No, I'm not.
```

```markdown
Is Mike ...?
Yes, he is.
Not, he is not. / No he isn't.
```

```markdown
Are they ...?
Yes, they are.
No, they are not. / No, they aren't.
```

## 24 含一般动词的疑问句 (主语是 you)

- 把 `do` 放到句首

```markdown
You like cats. -> Do you like cats?
```

## 25 含一般动词的疑问句 (主语是第三人称单数)

- 把 `does` 放到句首

```markdown
She likes cats. -> Does she like cats?
```
