# 疑问句

## 27 What 疑问句 (含 be 动词)

- 疑问句可以分为两大类：一种是回答 Yes/No 的一般疑问句；另一种是回答具体信息的特殊疑问句

- ...是什么翻译为 `What is...?`

- `What is` 可以缩写为 `What's`

- 对于 `What is ...?` 这种问句，要使用 `It is ...` 回答

```markdown
What is it? -> It is my book.
```

- 问句是 `What is this/that?` 也要用 `It is ...` 回答，而不是使用 `This/That is ...` 回答

## 28 询问时间/星期几

### 时间

- What time ... ?
- What's the time?
- Do you have the time?

```markdown
What time is it? 现在几点?
It's ten. / It's ten o'clock. / It's ten thirty.
```

### 星期几

- What day ... ?

```markdown
What day is it today? 今天星期几?
It's Monday.
```

### 日期

```markdown
What's the date today? 今天几月几号？
It's May 5 (fifth).
```

## 29 what 疑问句 (含一般动词)

- 格式 : What do/does ...?

```markdown
What do you have? -> I have ...
What does he have? -> He has ...
```

- 询问种类 : What kind of ...?

```markdown
What kind of music do you like? 你喜欢哪种类型的音乐?
```

## 30 who, where, when

- 规则与 what 一样

## 31 how

### 含义 1 : ...怎么样?

- How is 缩写是 How's

```markdown
How is Mike? -> How's Mike? 麦克过的好吗?
How is the weather? 天气怎么样？
```

### 含义 2 : 怎么做/用什么方法

```markdown
How do you come to school? 你如何来学校？
I take the bus. 我坐公交车来。
```

### be 动词过去式

- How was ...? 用来询问对事物的感想

```markdown
How was the movie? 电影怎么样？
```
