# Have to, Must

## 64 "必须"

- 要表示 "必须做..." 可以在动词前使用 `have to`
- `have to` 之后的动词为原形

```markdown
It's late now. I have to go home.
```

## 65 have to 的否定句和疑问句

- 在 `have to` 之前加上 `don't 或 doesn't` 可以形成否定句

```markdown
I have to go home. -> I don't have to go home.
She has to go home. -> She doesn't have to go home.
```

- 将 `do / does` 移动到句子开头形成疑问句

```markdown
Do you have to go home?
Does she have to go home?
```
