# Have to, Must

## 64 "必须"

- 要表示 "必须做..." 可以在动词前使用 `have to`
- `have to` 之后的动词为原形

```markdown
It's late now. I have to go home.
```

## 65 have to 的否定句和疑问句

- 在 `have to` 之前加上 `don't 或 doesn't` 可以形成否定句

```markdown
I have to go home. -> I don't have to go home.
She has to go home. -> She doesn't have to go home.
```

- 将 `do / does` 移动到句子开头形成疑问句

```markdown
Do you have to go home?
Does she have to go home?
```

## 66 "必须..."

- `must` 也可以表示 "必须..."

- 主语是第三人称单数时，`must` 的形态也不会改变

```markdown
I must complete my homework.
She must complete here homework.
```

- `must` 后面的动词为原形

```markdown
She must do it.
```

- `must not` 否定句，可以缩写为 `mustn't`，表示 "绝对不能"

```markdown
You must not move.
You mustn't move.
```

- `must not` 表示 "绝对不能"；`don't have to` 表示 "可以不"

```markdown
You must not eat it. 你绝对不能吃它
You don't have to eat it. 你可以不吃它
```

- `have to` 表示因为某个客观事实而必须做；`must` 表示说话者觉得必须要做
