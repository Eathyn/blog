# 一般将来时

## 53 表示将来的 be going to

- 在主语和动词中间加 `be going to` 表示将来要发生的事情，`be` 动词随着主语变化，动词保持原型

```markdown
I am going to play soccer tomorrow.
He is going to play soccer tomorrow.
```

## 54 be going to 的否定句和疑问句

- 在 `be going to` 的 `be` 动词之后加 `not` 形成否定句

```markdown
I am going to play basketball. -> I am not going to play basketball.
```

- 把 `be going to` 的 `be` 动词提到句首形成疑问句

```markdown
Are you going to play basketball? -> Yes, I am. / No, I am not.
```

- `be going to` 的否定句和疑问句不会用 `do` 或 `does`

```markdown
Is he going to come? // true
Does he going to come? // false
```

- 非正式口语中 `going to` 可以缩写为 `gonna`

```markdown
I am going to play basketball. -> I am gonna play basketball.
```

## 55 你要做什么

- 用 `What are you going to do?` 表达 "你将来要做什么?"，用 `I am going to ...` 回答

```markdown
What are you going to do? -> I am going to play basketball.
```

- `What` 也可以改为 `Where` / `How` / `When` 等词

```markdown
Where is he going to play the piano?
How long are you going to stay?
When are you going to do your homework?
```

- 用 `was/were going to...` 表达过去将来时，表示过去某个时间打算未来要做的某事

```markdown
I was going to the shop. 我当时打算去商店。
```

## 56 表示将来的 will

- 除了使用 `be going to` 表示将来时，也可以动词前加 `will`
- `will` 后的动词为原型

```markdown
I will complete my resume tonight.
She will be a good teacher.
```

- `will` 可以缩写

```markdown
I will -> I'll
He will -> He'll
```

- 计划、打算的事用 `be going to`；表示意愿的事用 `will`

```markdown
I am going to visit China next week. -> I will go with you.
```

## 57 will 的否定句和疑问句

- `will` + `not` 形成否定句
- `will not` 可缩写为 `won't`

```markdown
I will not study tonight.
I won't study tonight.
```

- `will` 的疑问句用 `Will` 开头

```markdown
Will you do homework tonight? -> Yes, I will. / No, I will not.
```

- 使用 `will` 的句子，动词用原形

```markdown
Will you be at home on Sunday? -> Yes, I will. / No, I won't.
```
